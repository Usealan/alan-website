Twilio A2P 10DLC Tips

Starting September 2023, Twilio will require all of its users with 10-digit phone numbers to register campaigns under their brand. SMS and MMS messages sent by unregistered phone numbers will be completely blocked after August 31, 2023.

We strongly recommend all our Certified Partners to take action as soon as possible to avoid any Twilio service disruptions in the near future. To learn more about this Twilio requirement, please login to your Twilio account and visit this link:

How do end-users consent to receive your messages?

Users opt-in by going to landing pages and entering their first name, last name, email, and phone number. They also check a box with the following label:

"By providing your phone number, you agree to receive text messages from business_name. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies."
